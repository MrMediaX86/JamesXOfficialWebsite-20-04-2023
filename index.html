Where Art Comes to Die

James X is an independent artist collective by new media artist Mr MediaX and filmmaker/writer Michael James focused on disrupting dominant narratives and pushing the limits of artistic meaning. At the heart of our project lies an investigation into the nature of the image through which we perceive reality, the gaze of the subject who creates the image, and ultimately into the performance of the position from which the subject gazes. We also have no clue what the f*** that means, but welcome to our online home and website.
